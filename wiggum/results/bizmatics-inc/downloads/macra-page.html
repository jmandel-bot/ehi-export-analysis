Real-World Testing

21st Century Cures Act has introduced 170.405 Real World Testing(RWT) as a part of Condition and Maintenance of Certification. HIT developers are required to test the certified EHR technology for interoperability to any one or more ONC Certifications for Healthcare IT Criteria, in the type of care settings in which it could be marketed.

Bizmatics submitting PrognoCIS RWT Test Plans and Test Results by the due dates to their ONC-ACB Drummond Group for approval. The approved RWT Test Plans and Test Results will be published on ONC CHPL (Certified Health IT Product List).

MIPS allows providers to earn payment adjustments in 2019 based on their reporting for three categories in 2017:

  1. Quality
  2. Improvement Activities &
  3. Advancing Care Information.

If you see more than 100 Medicare patients or bill more than $30,000 in Medicare Part B, you are eligible to participate in the Quality Payment Program.

What is MACRA?

The Medicare Access & CHIP Reauthorization Act, known as MACRA, presents a new value-based payment system to emphasize patient care. This program combines the Physician Quality Reporting System, Value-Based Modifier, and Meaningful Use to form the Quality Payment Program.

Medicare providers can participate through an Advanced Alternative Payment Model (APM) or the Merit-Based Incentive Payment System (MIPS). The Medicare Access and Chip Reauthorization Act of 2015 (MACRA) aims to reform healthcare by promoting a shift to value-based care.
