PR_EST_ADDL_INFO
Description:
Contains additional information related to a price estimate, such as tax and discount amounts.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ESTIMATE_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?

The name of the diagnosis.
