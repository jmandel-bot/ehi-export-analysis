PAT_ENC_CC_AUTO_CHG
Description:
This table contains data related to a patient's encounter, specifically the patient giving consent for the charges linked to this encounter to be automatically paid by the patient's credit card.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
3 CONSENT_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates if the patient gave consent to have a credit card charged for self-pay portion of visit.
May contain organization-specific values: No
