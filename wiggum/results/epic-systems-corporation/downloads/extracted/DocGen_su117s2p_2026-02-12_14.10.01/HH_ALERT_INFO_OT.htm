HH_ALERT_INFO_OT
Description:
This table stores the overtime information for Home Health Alerts.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ALERT_ID 1
CONTACT_DATE_REAL 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ALERT_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the alert record.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
4 NUM INTEGER No
This is the contact number for home health alerts.
5 OVERRIDE_NAME VARCHAR No
The user-configured name of the alert.
6 OVERRIDE_ACTIVE VARCHAR No
The override for the active status of the alert.
7 ALERT_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The status of the alert.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
CURRENT
PAST
8 REC_INTVL_OVERRIDE INTEGER No
The user-configured recurrence interval of the alert.
9 REMINDER_OFFSET INTEGER No
The user-configured reminder offset for the alert.
10 NUM_DAYS_ACTIVE_OVERRIDE INTEGER No
The user-configured number of days active for the alert.
11 HIGH_PRIORITY_OFFSET_OVERRIDE INTEGER No
The user-configured offset value for the alert.
12 ALERT_DUE_DATE DATETIME No
The default alert due date.
13 OVERRIDE_DUE_DATE DATETIME No
The user-configured alert due date.
14 NOTE_ID VARCHAR No
The ID of the note associated with the alert.
