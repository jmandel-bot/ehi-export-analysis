ORD_CV_FINDING
Description:
This table contains information about a single finding.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
CV_FINDING_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 CV_FINDING_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the finding record.
2 FORM_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
The contact date real (DTE) of the associated contact in the Forms (LQF) master file. This is a unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
3 WALL_STAGE INTEGER No
The stage number for wall motion scoring.
4 WALL_SCORE NUMERIC No
The average of the wall motion scoring.
5 WALL_NORMAL NUMERIC No
The normal index of the wall motion scoring.
6 WALL_MOTION_VIEW_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The wall motion views select category ID for the finding record.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
LAO
RAO
Both
7 FINDING_SEQUENCE NUMERIC No
Defines a relative order among result records (RES) in a report segment. For instance, a record with a value of 5 in this item will appear earlier in the report segment than a record with a value of 7 in this item.
