dbo.SXAAMLanguageAbilityProficiency 
Default Table Name  SXAAMLanguageAbilityProficiency 
Definition  Stores all the configured abilities with with which a patient may be able to communicate. This will always be data-loaded, but in the future users may have the option to modify codes or inactivate and add rows.
