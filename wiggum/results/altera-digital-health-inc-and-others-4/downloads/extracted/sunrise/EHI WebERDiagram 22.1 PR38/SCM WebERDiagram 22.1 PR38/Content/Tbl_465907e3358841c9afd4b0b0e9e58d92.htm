dbo.SXAMMCartFillPolicyFillingDetail 
Default Table Name  SXAMMCartFillPolicyFillingDetail 
Definition  This table will hold Filling Details like Batch time, exchange time , from weekday and time and to weekday and time for the non traditional cartfill policy. A cartfill policy may have several rows of data in this table.
