dbo.CV3OrderAvailabilityPolicyDtl 
Default Table Name  CV3OrderAvailabilityPolicyDtl 
Definition  Defines the locations that are or are not available when the policy is used. The detail rows identify which locations are (or are not) available when the policy is used. It can also control which order entry form will be used when the order is entered from the location. The availability policy can be associated to an order catalog item, prefilled item or order set/pathway.
