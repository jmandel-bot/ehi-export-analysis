dbo.SXARCMLateChargeProcessingRequest 
Default Table Name  SXARCMLateChargeProcessingRequest 
Definition  This table will be populated with the episode and transaction info for all late charges entered in the system and will be used by the financials windows service job worker to trigger the late charge processing.
