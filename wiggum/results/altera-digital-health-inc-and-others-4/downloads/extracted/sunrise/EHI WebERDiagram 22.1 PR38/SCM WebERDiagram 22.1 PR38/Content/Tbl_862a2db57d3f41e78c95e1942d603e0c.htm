dbo.CV3HealthIssueDeclTemplate 
Default Table Name  CV3HealthIssueDeclTemplate 
Definition  Template of a health issue. This allows health issues to be entered from the Knowledge Tree. When entered from the Knowledge Tree (client document), this template is used to provide default values.
