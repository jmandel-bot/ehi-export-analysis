dbo.SXAESMasterCalendar 
Default Table Name  SXAESMasterCalendar 
Definition  Master Calendars allow administrators to detail the schedules when a resource in scheduling is available for appointments and where and how many appointments they may except during any specific time. This table contains the main record for a Master Calendar. Other tables will detail the availability of the resource and attach this record to the resource itself.
