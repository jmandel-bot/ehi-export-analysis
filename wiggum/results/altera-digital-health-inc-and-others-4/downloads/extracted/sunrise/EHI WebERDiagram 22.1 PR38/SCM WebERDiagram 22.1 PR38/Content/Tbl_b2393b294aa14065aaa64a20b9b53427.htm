dbo.SXAHMClientEventActionStatusHistory 
Default Table Name  SXAHMClientEventActionStatusHistory 
Definition  This Health Management patient data table tracks the history of actions that resulted in a change to the status of the entices series of Client Events, which is equivalent to a row on the patient's schedule.
