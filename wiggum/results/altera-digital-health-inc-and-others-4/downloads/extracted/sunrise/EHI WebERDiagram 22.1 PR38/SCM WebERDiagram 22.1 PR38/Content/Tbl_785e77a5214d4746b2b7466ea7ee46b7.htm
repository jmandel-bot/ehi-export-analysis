dbo.SXAAMLocnRelatedLocnXREF 
Default Table Name  SXAAMLocnRelatedLocnXREF 
Definition  This table will contain relationships between facilities 72 Hour Warnings configured by the user. Based upon Medicare billing criteria, a check must be performed for an inpatient visit registration to see any out-patient visits occurred for this patient in the current or related facility 72 hours prior to the inpatient visit, so long as the patient’s insurance is marked to follow the 72 hour rule warning.
