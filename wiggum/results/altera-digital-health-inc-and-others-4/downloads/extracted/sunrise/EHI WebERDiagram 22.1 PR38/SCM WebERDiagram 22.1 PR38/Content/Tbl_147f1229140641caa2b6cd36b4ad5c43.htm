dbo.SXAMMPharmacyBackupUnit 
Default Table Name  SXAMMPharmacyBackupUnit 
Definition  Backup pharmacy routing by units served feature (part of 'Label Routing-Workflow Improvements'). This table will store units served by a backup pharmacy. So relationship between SXAMMPharmacyBackup and SXAMMPharmacyBackupUnit will be similar to the one between SXAMMPharmacy and SXAMMPharmacyUnit.
