dbo.SXAESHolidayLocationXREF 
Default Table Name  SXAESHolidayLocationXREF 
Definition  Per the specification, holidays are to be "applied" to various locations in the application, which will then raise exceptions on the appropriate Master Calendars. This table will be needed to track which locations a holiday has been applied to.
