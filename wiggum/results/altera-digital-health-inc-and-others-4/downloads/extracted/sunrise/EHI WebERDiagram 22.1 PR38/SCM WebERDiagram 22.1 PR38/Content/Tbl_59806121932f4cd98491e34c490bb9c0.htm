dbo.SXAHMEducationalMaterialType 
Default Table Name  SXAHMEducationalMaterialType 
Definition  This Health Management configuration dictionary defines the types of Educational Material that are available. If the Education Material is a Vaccine Information Sheet, this will be indicated by the IsVIS flag.
