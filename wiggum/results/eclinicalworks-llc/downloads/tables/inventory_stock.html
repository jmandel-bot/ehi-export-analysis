Table inventory_stock
Description This table stores information about the inventory stock of products in a facility.
Column DataType Description
id (Primary Key) int Unique identifier for each inventory stock record.
Product_id int identifier for the product, refers to the product table's primary key.
Facility_id int identifier for the facility where the product is stored, refers to the facility table's primary key.
SKU int Stock Keeping Unit, a unique identifier for each distinct product.
ExpiryDate datetime The date when the product expires.
SerialNo varchar The serial number of the product.
OrderQty decimal The quantity of the product ordered.
UnitQty decimal The quantity of the product in units.
UsageQty decimal The quantity of the product used.
IsDeleted tinyint A flag indicating if the inventory stock record is deleted (1) or not (0).
UpdatedBy int identifier for the user who last updated the record, refers to the user table's primary key.
UpdatedOn datetime The date and time when the record was last updated.
IsTransfered tinyint A flag indicating if the inventory stock is transferred (1) or not (0).
Sale_id int identifier for the sale associated with the inventory stock, refers to the sale table's primary key.
CreatedBy int identifier for the user who created the record, refers to the user table's primary key.
CreatedOn datetime The date and time when the record was created.
Encid int Encrypted identifier for the inventory stock record.
BaseCurve varchar The base curve of the product (specific to contact lenses).
Diameter varchar The diameter of the product (specific to contact lenses).
Color varchar The color of the product.
Tint varchar The tint of the product.
Transition varchar The transition of the product.
Sph varchar The spherical power of the product (specific to contact lenses).
Cyl varchar The cylindrical power of the product (specific to contact lenses).
Axis varchar The axis of the product (specific to contact lenses).
AddStr varchar The additional strength of the product (specific to contact lenses).
HPrism varchar The horizontal prism of the product (specific to contact lenses).
VPrism varchar The vertical prism of the product (specific to contact lenses).
Stockreason_id int identifier for the reason of the stock update, refers to the stock reason table's primary key.
note varchar A note about the inventory stock record.
isStockUpdated tinyint A flag indicating if the stock is updated (1) or not (0).
mrp decimal The maximum retail price of the product.
mrp_abated decimal The abated maximum retail price of the product.
rate decimal The rate of the product.
billedQty decimal The billed quantity of the product.
freeQty decimal The free quantity of the product.
DiscountPerLineInvoice decimal The discount percentage per line in the invoice.
DiscountTypeInvoice int The type of discount applied in the invoice.
DiscValueInvoice decimal The discount value applied in the invoice.
vatPercent decimal The VAT percentage applied to the product.
netValue decimal The net value of the product.
totalPrice decimal The total price of the product.
rackno varchar The rack number where the product is stored.
pack decimal The pack size of the product.
vatAmt decimal The VAT amount applied to the product.
isVatOnMRP tinyint A flag indicating if VAT is applied on MRP (1) or not (0).
productinvoice_id int identifier for the product invoice, refers to the product invoice table's primary key.
singleVatAmt decimal The single VAT amount applied to the product.
source tinyint The source of the inventory stock record.
Currency varchar The currency used for the product prices, e.g., USD.
isFreeQtyIncluded tinyint A flag indicating if the free quantity is included (1) or not (0).
isdiscarded tinyint A flag indicating if the inventory stock is discarded (1) or not (0).
supplier_id int identifier for the supplier of the product, refers to the supplier table's primary key.
UnitCost decimal The unit cost of the product.
preferencecardmap_id int identifier for the preference card map, refers to the preference card map table's primary key.
reclocked tinyint A flag indicating if the inventory stock is locked for receiving (1) or not (0).
LotNo varchar The lot number of the product.
BilledUnitCost decimal The billed unit cost of the product.
Contactlens_id int identifier for the contact lens, refers to the contact lens table's primary key.
ReceivedQty decimal The received quantity of the product.
