dbo.SXAESMCDefinitionAvailability 
Default Table Name  SXAESMCDefinitionAvailability 
Definition  This table stores the actual availability definitions for the Master Calendar, these are the time periods and availability types that the calendar has during the day.

The format is the Build number followed by 3 digits for the patch level which is typically 000.
