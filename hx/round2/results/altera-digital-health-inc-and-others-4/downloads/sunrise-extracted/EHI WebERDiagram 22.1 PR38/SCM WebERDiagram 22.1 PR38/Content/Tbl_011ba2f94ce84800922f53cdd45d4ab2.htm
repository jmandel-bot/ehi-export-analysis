dbo.SXAAMFindPatientCriteriaSource 
Default Table Name  SXAAMFindPatientCriteriaSource 
Definition  The purpose of SXAAMFindPatientCriteriaSource is to allow partners to configure plug-ins for a new tab in Find Patient that allows them to gather search criteria by some other way than manual entry (eg. swiping a card).
