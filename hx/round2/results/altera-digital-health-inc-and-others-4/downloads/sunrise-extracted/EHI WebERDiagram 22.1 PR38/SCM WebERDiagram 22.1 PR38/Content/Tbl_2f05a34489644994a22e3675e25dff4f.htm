dbo.CV3FlowsheetVersionItem 
Default Table Name  CV3FlowsheetVersionItem 
Definition  Defines the individual observation item and headings for a flowsheet configuration. When adding observations to flowsheets (can only add a observation to a master flowsheet), the entries are inserted into this table. The entries in this table are related one to one with the entries in CV3ObservationEntryItem.
