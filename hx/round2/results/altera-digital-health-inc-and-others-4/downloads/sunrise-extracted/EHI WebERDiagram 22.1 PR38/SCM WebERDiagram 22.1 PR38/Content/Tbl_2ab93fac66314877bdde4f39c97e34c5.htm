dbo.SXAAMBAllergyCategoryDrugXRef 
Default Table Name  SXAAMBAllergyCategoryDrugXRef 
Definition  For the Ambulatory Rx Writer, allergy interactions are raised at 3 levels – Drug, Category, and Class. This table defines the relationship between a drug and its allergy category.
