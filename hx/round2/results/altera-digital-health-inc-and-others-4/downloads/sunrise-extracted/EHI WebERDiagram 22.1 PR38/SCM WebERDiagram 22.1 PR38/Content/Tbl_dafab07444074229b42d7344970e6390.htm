dbo.SXACCState 
Default Table Name  SXACCState 
Definition  Travel history will consist of ISO 3166-2 and FIPS 5-2 states and this table will store the list of states/provinces to be dispayed in travel history dialog. It will be synced using ATP content using existing TermSync job.
