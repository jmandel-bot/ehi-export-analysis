dbo.SXAAMThirdPartyVerification 
Default Table Name  SXAAMThirdPartyVerification 
Definition  SXAAMThirdPartyVerification allows for the configuring of a third party plug-in that may be invoked at certain points within the AM workflows for the purposes of verifying and updating patient/visit data.
