dbo.SXAMMPharmacyStockInventoryTransaction 
Default Table Name  SXAMMPharmacyStockInventoryTransaction 
Definition  This entity contains the manual transactions that adjust the inventory of generic items in the pharmacy

This includes adjustments for receiving orders, initial quantities, correction of mistakes, etc.
