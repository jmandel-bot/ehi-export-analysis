dbo.SXASRGSurgicalItemCategory 
Default Table Name  SXASRGSurgicalItemCategory 
Definition  This table contains the surgical item category dictionary entries. This will be used to classify the surgical item by categories. The internal types for these entries will be Equipments, Devices and Instruments, Supplies, Trays, Implants, and Tissues.
