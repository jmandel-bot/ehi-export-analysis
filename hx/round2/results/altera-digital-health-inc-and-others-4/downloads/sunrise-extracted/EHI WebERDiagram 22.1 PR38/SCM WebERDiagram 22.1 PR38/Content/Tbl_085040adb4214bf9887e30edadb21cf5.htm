dbo.SXACDFSParamFanoutXRef 
Default Table Name  SXACDFSParamFanoutXRef 
Definition  This table is used to store the fanout mappings of Structured Notes parameters to Flowsheet parameters and Flowsheet parameters to Flowsheet parameters.
