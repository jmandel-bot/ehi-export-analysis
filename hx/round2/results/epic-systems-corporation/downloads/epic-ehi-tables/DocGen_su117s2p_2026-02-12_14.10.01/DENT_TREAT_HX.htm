DENT_TREAT_HX
Description:
This table contains history items of changes in dental treatments.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
TREATMENT_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 TREATMENT_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the treatment plan record
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 TPL_MOD_INSTANT_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Stores date and time for when the dental treatment was modified.
4 TPL_MOD_USER_ID VARCHAR No
User who modifies data in a treatment record
5 TPL_MOD_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
6 TR_LINKED_CSN NUMERIC No
Stores the patient encounter in which a particular piece of the treatment record was modified if it was available.
7 TR_COMP_INST_HIST_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Stores the revision history for the completed date and time of a treatment
8 TR_STATUS_HX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
stores the revision history of treatment status
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Planned
Completed
Canceled
Draft
Discontinued
9 TR_START_DATE_HIST_DT DATETIME No
stores the revision history for start date for a treatment
10 TR_ORDER_NUM_HX INTEGER No
stores revision history of sequence number of a treatment
11 DENT_SER_HX_ID_PROV_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider. This item may be hidden in a public view of the CLARITY_SER table.
12 TREAT_CHRON_INSTANT_DTTM DATETIME (Attached) No
This column records the date and time of a documentation change to the dental treatment.
13 DENT_OPT_STAT_HX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The history of changes to the status of a treatment option within a dental treatment.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Selected
Unselected
Removed
14 DENT_HB_ESTIMATE_YN VARCHAR No
This flag indicates whether the an estimate was calculated for the dental treatment option at a given time.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
15 DENT_HB_GUARANTOR_ID NUMERIC No
The guarantor used when calculating estimates for the dental treatment option. This only applies to Hospital Billing (HB) estimates.
16 DENT_HB_PRIMARY_CVG_ID NUMERIC No
The primary coverage used when calculating the dental treatment option's estimate. This only applies to Hospital Billing (HB) estimates.
17 TR_APRV_DTTM_HX_DTTM DATETIME (Attached) No
The revision history for the date and time when the treatment was approved.
18 TR_APRV_USER_HX_ID VARCHAR No
The revision history containing the unique ID for the user who approved the treatment.
19 TR_APRV_USER_HX_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
20 TR_APRV_STATUS_HX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The revision history of the approval status category ID for the treatment.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Unapproved
Submitted
Approved
Rescinded
Modified after Approval
21 TR_RSCND_RSN_HX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The revision history for the rescind reason category ID for the treatment.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Approved in Error
Change in Patient Condition
