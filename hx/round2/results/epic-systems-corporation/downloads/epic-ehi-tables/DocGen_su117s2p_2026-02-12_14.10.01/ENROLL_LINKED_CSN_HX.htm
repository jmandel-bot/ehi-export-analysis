ENROLL_LINKED_CSN_HX
Description:
This table stores history information about encounters linked to or unlinked from research enrollments.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ENROLL_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ENROLL_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the enrollment record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 HX_PRL_CSN NUMERIC No
History information of study protocols linked to the encounter's contact serial numbers (CSN).
4 HX_LNK_UNIQ_DAY_NUM INTEGER No
History information of the timeline day linked to this encounter. This value corresponds to the value in ENROLL_TIMELINE.UNIQ_DAY_NUM.
5 HX_TPL_CSN NUMERIC No
History information of the treatment plan contact serial number (CSN) linked to the encounter.
6 HX_TREATMENTDAY_CSN NUMERIC No
History information of the treatment day contact serial number (CSN) linked to the encounter.
7 HX_WHO_LINK_ENC_ID VARCHAR No
History information of the user who linked or unlinked an encounter to a study.
8 HX_WHO_LINK_ENC_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
9 HX_WHEN_LINK_ENC_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
History information of UTC formatted instant when user links or unlinks an encounter to a study.
10 HX_LINKENC_ACTION_C_NAME VARCHAR No
History information of what action the user takes on encounter-study linkage.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Linked
Unlinked
11 HX_LINKED_ENC_CSN NUMERIC No
History information of contact serial numbers (CSN) of encounters linked to the study.
12 HX_TREATMENT_DAY_LINE INTEGER No
History information of the line number of the treatment day in the treatment plan that this encounter is linked to.
13 HX_LINK_ENC_SOURCE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
History information about the method used to link or unlink an encounter to or from a study. Blank is an unspecified process (before the data was collected)
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Manual Action
Automatic Action
Signing Order
Releasing Child Order
Data Conversion
