PAT_ENC_EXT_HOSP
Description:
This table contains information on external hospital admission encounters. The information includes the admission and discharge dates along with the admitting hospital.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 PAT_ENC_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique, internal contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of the contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
4 EXT_HOSP_NAME_LOC_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the revenue location.
5 HOSP_ADMIT_DATE DATETIME No
Date the patient was admitted to the external hospital.
6 HOSP_DISCHRG_DATE DATETIME No
Date the patient was discharged from the hospital.
7 CONTACT_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The encounter type category number for the encounter.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
