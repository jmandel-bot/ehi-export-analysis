DENTAL_HB_EST_HX
Description:
Dental procedure estimates history as calculated from the Hospital Billing (HB) estimates application programming interface (API).

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
FINDING_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 FINDING_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the finding record
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 DENT_HX_CALC_PRICE NUMERIC No
The historical price of a dental procedure as calculated by the Hospital Billing application
4 DENT_HX_ADJ_PRICE NUMERIC No
The historical user-adjusted price of the dental procedure (Hospital Billing estimates only)
5 DENT_HX_ADJ_REASN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The historical reason for a price adjustment for a dental procedure (Hospital Billing estimates only)
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Overridden from Enterprise Billing
Preserved from Previous Estimate
Unsuccessful Attempt
6 DENT_HX_ADJ_COMMENT VARCHAR No
The historical comment for the price adjustment for a dental procedure (Hospital Billing estimates only)
7 DENT_HX_ADJ_USER_ID VARCHAR No
Historical user that adjusted the estimate (Hospital Billing estimates only)
8 DENT_HX_ADJ_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
9 DENT_HX_PAT_PORTION NUMERIC No
Stores the historical patient portion of a dental procedure's price (used for Hospital Billing estimates only)
10 DENT_HX_OUTDATED_YN VARCHAR No
Stores whether a historical estimate value is flagged as out of date (used for Hospital Billing estimates only)
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
