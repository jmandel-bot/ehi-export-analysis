MEM_LIST_REPL
Description:
This table contains member context information for capitation transactions.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
MEM_LIST_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 MEM_LIST_ID NUMERIC No
The ID of the transaction member list record computed as part of the specialty capitation payment process.
2 EFFECTIVE_DATE DATETIME No
The effective date of the specialty capitation payment computation.
3 ELIGIBILITY_DATE DATETIME No
The test date used to determine if a member is eligible for inclusion in specialty capitation payment computations.
4 SERV_AREA_ID_LOC_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the revenue location.
5 CARRIER_ID VARCHAR No
The ID of the carrier that defines the context of the specialty capitation payment computation.
6 CARRIER_ID_CARRIER_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the carrier record.
7 NETWORK_ID VARCHAR No
The ID of the network that defines the context of the specialty capitation payment computation.
8 NETWORK_ID_NETWORK_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the network.
9 BENEFIT_PLAN_ID_BENEFIT_PLAN_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the benefit plan record.
10 RKP_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the risk panel for the member.
11 RKP_ID_RISK_PANEL_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the risk panel.
12 LOB_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the line of business for the member.
13 LOB_ID_LOB_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the line of business record.
14 MGRP_ID_MEM_GRP_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the member group
15 MEM_LIST_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The type of member list that this record represents.
May contain organization-specific values: No

The ID of the transaction member list that this list adjusts.
17 LOAD_BATCH_DATE DATETIME No
The date that the member list was loaded into the system.
18 ASSOC_EXT_VAL_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The member group association value for the member list.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
19 CAPITATION_CODE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The capitation code associated with the transaction.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
20 PRODUCT_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The product type associated with the member list.
