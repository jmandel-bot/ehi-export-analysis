CL_RMT_PRV_SUP_INF
Description:
This table contains the provider supplemental summary information for the remittance record.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
IMAGE_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 IMAGE_ID VARCHAR No
This is the ID for the remittance image record with related remit claim references.
2 TOT_DRG_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total diagnosis related group amount for the remittance record.
3 FED_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total federal-specific amount for the remittance record.
4 HOSP_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total hospital-specific amount for the remittance record.
5 DISP_SHARE_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total disproportionate share amount for the remittance record.
6 TOT_CAP_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total capital amount for the remittance record.
7 MED_EDU_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total indirect medical education amount for the remittance record.
8 TOT_OUT_DAY_CNT NUMERIC No
This is the total number of outlier days for the remittance record.
9 DAY_OUT_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total day outlier amount for the remittance record.
10 COST_OUT_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total cost outlier amount for the remittance record.
11 AVG_DRG_LEN_STAY INTEGER No
This is the diagnosis related group (DRG) average length of stay for the remittance record.
12 TOT_DISCHARGE_CNT INTEGER No
This is the total number of discharges for the remittance record.
13 COST_REP_DAY_CNT INTEGER No
This is the total number of cost report days for the remittance record.
14 CVRD_DAY_CNT INTEGER No
This is the total number of covered days for the remittance record.
15 NONCVRD_DAY_CNT INTEGER No
This is the total number of non-covered days for the remittance record.
16 MSP_PASS_THRU_AMT NUMERIC No
This is the total Medicare Secondary Payer (MSP) pass- through amount calculated for a non-Medicare payer for the remittance record.
17 AVG_DRG_WEIGHT NUMERIC No
This is the average diagnosis-related group (DRG) weight for the remittance record.
18 PPS_CAP_FSP_DRG_AM NUMERIC No
This is the total prospective payment system (PPS) capital, federal-specific portion, diagnosis-related group (DRG) amount for the remittance record.
19 PPS_CAP_HSP_DRG NUMERIC No
This is the total prospective payment system (PPS) capital, hospital-specific portion, diagnosis-related group (DRG) amount for the remittance record.
20 TOT_PPS_DSH_DRG_AM NUMERIC No
This is the total prospective payment system (PPS) disproportionate share, hospital diagnosis-related group (DRG) amount for the remittance record.
