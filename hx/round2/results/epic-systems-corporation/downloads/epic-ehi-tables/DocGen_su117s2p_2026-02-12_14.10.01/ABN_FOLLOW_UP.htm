ABN_FOLLOW_UP
Description:
This table stores the data related to the follow up done on an Advanced Beneficiary Notice (ABN).

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
NOTE_CSN_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 NOTE_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The contact serial number (CSN) of the contact.
2 NOTE_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the note (HNO) record that contains the Advance Beneficiary Notice (ABN) information.
3 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 CONTACT_NUM VARCHAR No
The number of this contact.
6 ABN_FLUP_USER_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the user who did the follow up on this ABN
7 ABN_FLUP_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
8 ABN_FOLLOW_UP_INST_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Stores the instant at which the follow up was done.
9 ABN_FLUP_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the status of the Advance Beneficiary Notice (ABN) follow-up.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
