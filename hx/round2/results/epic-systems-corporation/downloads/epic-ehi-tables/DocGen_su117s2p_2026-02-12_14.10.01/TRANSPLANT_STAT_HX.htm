TRANSPLANT_STAT_HX
Description:
This table contains information about a transplant episode's phase, status, and reason.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID NUMERIC No
The unique ID of the episode of care record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
Each line represents a change in status for this transplant episode.
3 TX_STAGE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The phase of the transplant episode, e.g. Pre-Evaluation, Evaluation, Transplanted.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Referral
Evaluation
Waitlist
Admitted for Transplant
Transplanted
4 TX_STAT_DT DATETIME No
Effective date associated with the patient's transplant phase, status, and reason.
5 TX_STATUS_OUT_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Status associated with the patient's transplant phase and reason.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes

6 TX_STAT_UPD_USER_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the user who updated the transplant phase, status, or reason.
7 TX_STAT_UPD_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
8 TX_STAT_UPD_INST DATETIME (Local) No
The instant the transplant phase, status, or reason was updated.

10 TXP_PHASE_ENC_CSN NUMERIC No
Contact Serial Number (CSN) for the encounter where the transplant phase, status, or reason was changed. This is used in the transplant history audit trail. It is also used to provide a link to the encounter in which the phase was changed.
