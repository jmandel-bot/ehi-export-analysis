ORDER_RAD_DICTATE
Description:
This table stores the dictation radiologist & dictating date information for orders performed in radiology.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORDER_PROC_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ORDER_PROC_ID NUMERIC No
The unique ID of the procedure order record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line count for this table as determined by the number of dictating radiologists for an order.
3 PROV_ID_PROV_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider. This item may be hidden in a public view of the CLARITY_SER table.
4 DICTATING_DT DATETIME No
The date that the radiologist sent the dictation to the transcriptionist.
5 DICTATED_UTC_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
This item captures the date and time in the Universal Time Coordinated (UTC) format that the provider listed in the Dictated Physicians (I ORD 52205) item dictated the study.
