HH_VO_ENC
Description:
Contains Home Health verbal order overtime single items information.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
VERBAL_ORDER_ID 1
CONTACT_DATE_REAL 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 VERBAL_ORDER_ID VARCHAR No
Unique identifier for the verbal order.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
Unique identifier for this contact for this patient.
3 CONTACT_NUMBER VARCHAR No
Contact number.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 CM_CT_OWNER_ID VARCHAR No
ID of the contact owner deployment for this record. Links to table CL_COMMUNTY_INSTNC.
6 INST_OF_EDIT DATETIME (Local) No
Verbal order instant of edit.
7 ENTRY_PERSON_ID VARCHAR No
Name of user who entered contact data. Links to table CLARITY_EMP.
8 ENTRY_PERSON_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
