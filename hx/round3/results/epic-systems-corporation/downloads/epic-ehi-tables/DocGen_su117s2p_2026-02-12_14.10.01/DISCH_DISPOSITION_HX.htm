DISCH_DISPOSITION_HX
Description:
This is the discharge disposition history table. It will track the changes made to the patient's discharge disposition throughout the encounter. This table contains the data about the discharge disposition (I EPT 11100), the user that made the change (I EPT 11101), the date and time that the discharge disposition was entered (I EPT 11102), and the source of the update to discharge disposition (I EPT 11104).

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the patient record for this row. This column is frequently used to link to the PATIENT table.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 CM_CT_OWNER_ID VARCHAR No
The Community ID (CID) of the instance that owns this contact. This is only populated if you use IntraConnect.
6 DISCH_DISP_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores a history of the discharge disposition.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
7 DISCH_DISP_USER_ID VARCHAR No
Stores a history of users who updated the discharge disposition.
8 DISCH_DISP_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
9 DISCH_DISP_INST_LOCAL_DTTM DATETIME (Attached) No
Stores a history of when the discharge disposition was updated.
10 DISCH_DISP_INST_UTC_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
Stores a history of when the discharge disposition was updated in UTC.
11 DISCH_DISP_UPD_SOURCE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The source of a change to the discharge disposition.
May contain organization-specific values: No
