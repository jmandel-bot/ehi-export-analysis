PAT_SVC_PROV_LIST_RECPNT
Description:
This table stores information of service provider list recipients that received the list through a guest link.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the patient record for this row. This column is frequently used to link to the PATIENT table.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 CM_CT_OWNER_ID VARCHAR No
The Community ID (CID) of the instance that owns this contact. This is only populated if you use IntraConnect.
6 LIST_IDENT INTEGER No
The unique ID in I EPT 97216 for the list that the recipient information is for.
7 RECIPIENT_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider list recipient.
8 RECIPIENT_CONTACT_METHOD_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The contact method used to send the service provider list to the recipient.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Text
Email
Both
9 RECIPIENT_PHONE VARCHAR No
The phone number that was used to send the service provider list to the recipient.
10 RECIPIENT_EMAIL VARCHAR No
The email that was used to send the service provider list to the recipient.
11 RECIPIENT_IS_NEW_CONTACT_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether the recipient of a Patient Choice list is a new visit contact created when sharing the list.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
