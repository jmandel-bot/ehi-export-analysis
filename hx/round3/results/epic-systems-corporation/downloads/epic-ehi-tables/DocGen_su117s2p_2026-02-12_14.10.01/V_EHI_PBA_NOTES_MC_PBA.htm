V_EHI_PBA_NOTES_MC_PBA
Description:
This view filters out account data that is not appropriate for member level EHI exports when group billing is used.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
NOTE_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 NOTE_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier of a note attached to the account.
2 NOTE_DATE DATETIME No
The date when the note was created. This column contains date information only. Column NOTE_DATETIME holds date and time information.
3 NOTE_USER_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier of the user that created the note.
4 NOTE_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
5 PB_ACCT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier of the Premium Billing account.
6 NOTE_LOCAL_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
The date and time the note was created.
