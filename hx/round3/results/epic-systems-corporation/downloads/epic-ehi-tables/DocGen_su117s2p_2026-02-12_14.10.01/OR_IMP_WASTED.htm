OR_IMP_WASTED
Description:
The OR_IMP_WASTED table contains OR management system implant waste information.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
IMPLANT_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 IMPLANT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the implant record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The number of the line of the waste information for the implant.
3 WASTED_DATE DATETIME No
The date that the implant was wasted.
4 WASTE_LOG_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the log in which the implant was wasted.
5 WASTE_NOTIFY_DATE DATETIME No
The date the manufacturer was notified that the implant was wasted.
6 REASON_WASTED_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The category value of the reason the implant was wasted.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
7 WASTED_TIME DATETIME (Local) No
The exact time an implant was marked as wasted.
