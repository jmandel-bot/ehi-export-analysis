dbo.SXAHMVaccineMedicationStockItem 
Default Table Name  SXAHMVaccineMedicationStockItem 
Definition  This Health Management configuration table defines the stock, including lot number, expiration, and source, of specific products for a given brand and packaging of a vaccine medication.
