dbo.SXAMMCartFillUnit 
Default Table Name  SXAMMCartFillUnit 
Definition  This entity contains a list of units that are assigned to a policy. Units are assigned to the same policy if their cart fill details are the same (exchange time cart fill period, print details, etc).
