dbo.CV3AncillaryOrderID 
Default Table Name  CV3AncillaryOrderID 
Definition  A cross-reference table that is used only by the interfaces that stores either the placer or filler information of existing orders to distinguish if the order was created by the interfaces or Sunrise Clinical Manager.
