PAT_ENC_DIR_UNAVAIL_RSN
Description:
Reasons why an appointment was unavailable for direct scheduling.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 DIR_UNAVAIL_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
List of reasons why this appointment could not have been scheduled online in Direct Scheduling.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Provider not enabled
Department not enabled
Visit type is not enabled
Provider not available for patient
Warnings overruled during scheduling
No patient scheduling access
Appointment request not sent to patient
Sequential appointments are not supported
Panel appointments are not supported
