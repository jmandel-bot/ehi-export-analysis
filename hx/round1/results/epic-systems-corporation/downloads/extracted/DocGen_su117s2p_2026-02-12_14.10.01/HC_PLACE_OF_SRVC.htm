HC_PLACE_OF_SRVC
Description:
This table contains home care place of service information.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID 1
LINE 2

Name Type Discontinued?

The GEO_RATING rates the accuracy of latitude and longitude for a given place of service address.
