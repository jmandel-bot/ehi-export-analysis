APPEAL_GRV_CHANGE_URGENCY
Description:
Stores the change urgency requests for an appeal/grievance. Each row represents a change urgency request.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
APPEAL_GRV_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 APPEAL_GRV_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the appeal/grievance record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 REQUESTED_URGENCY_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The requested urgency category ID of the change urgency request.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Standard
Expedited
4 REQUEST_UTC_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
The date and time in UTC that the change in urgency was requested.
5 REQUEST_LOCAL_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
The date and time that the change in urgency was requested.
6 INITIATED_BY_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The change urgency requested by type category ID for the change urgency request.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Provider
Member
Representative
Health Plan
Place of Service
7 URGENCY_COMMENTS VARCHAR No
The comments entered on the change urgency request.
8 URGENCY_DECISION_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The change urgency decison category ID made on the urgency change request.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Approved
Denied
