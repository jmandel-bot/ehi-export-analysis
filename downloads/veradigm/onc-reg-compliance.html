      21st Century Cures Final Rule Communications Compliance

      At Veradigm, we take regulatory compliance very seriously. As such, we outline below the communications guidelines that comply with the ONC's 21st Century Cures Act final rule.

      Should there be any restriction that contravenes the following communications restrictions in our contracts, we will not enforce them. Moreover, the next time a contract is renewed, any such restrictions (if present) will be removed.

      Veradigm will never prohibit or restrict any communication regarding:

      • The usability of our products;
      • The interoperability of our products;
      • The security of our products;
      • Relevant information regarding users' experiences when using our products;
      • Veradigm business practices related to exchanging electronic health information; or
      • The manner in which a user of a Veradigm product has used such product.
      File ONC Information Blocking Concern

      Veradigm is fully committed to supporting an interoperable healthcare environment, including partnering with clients, policy makers and other stakeholders to ensure information exchange occurs where appropriate. We are committed to addressing any concerns about information blocking, whether from clients or those submitted by others in a position to interact with Electronic Health Information documented in and exchanged from Veradigm technologies. If you would like to share feedback with Veradigm or request follow-up, please complete the form below and someone will contact you to gather more details in our effort to assess the concern and resolve where necessary.

      NOTE: Veradigm clients should avail themselves of our Client Support process or their Account Executive team if they have concerns or questions about something that might fall under the information blocking regulation. We will be happy to address any issues or questions in that way, as all Veradigm staff have undergone training on this important regulation.

      Intervention Risk Management Summary - Predictive Decision Support Interventions

      Veradigm's Intervention Risk Management Practices

      At Veradigm, we prioritize the safety, reliability, and effectiveness of our Predictive Decision Support Interventions (PDSIs) by adhering to comprehensive Intervention Risk Management (IRM) practices. These practices are designed to meet the stringent guidelines set forth by the ASTP/ONC and align with relevant sections of the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) AI Risk Management Framework. In addition, it aligns with FAVES principles of being fair, appropriate, valid, effective, and safe for all users.

      Our approach begins with a risk analysis, where a cross-functional team evaluates potential risks and adverse impacts associated with the validity, reliability, robustness, fairness, intelligibility, safety, security, and privacy of our predictive models. This helps to ensure that our models are accurate, consistent, resilient, unbiased, transparent, safe, secure, and protective of patient privacy.

      To address any identified risks, we implement risk mitigation strategies. These include regular updates and validation of our predictive models, ongoing monitoring and improvement of model performance, and the implementation of safeguards to reduce the risk of adverse impacts on patient care.

      Governance is a key aspect of our IRM practices. We enforce policies and controls to ensure that data is acquired ethically and legally, managed with high standards of integrity, and used responsibly to support accurate and fair predictions.

      To ensure that our practices remain up-to-date and effective, we review each release for Predictive Decision Support Interventions as well as conduct an annual review of our Predictive Decision Support Interventions and IRM practices. This allows us to update our documentation and practices as necessary, reflecting the latest standards and ensuring ongoing improvement.

      Veradigms IRM practices help ensure that its Predictive Decision Support Interventions align to applicable laws and regulations including ASTP/ONC certification criteria and the NIST AI Risk Management Framework.

Veradigm is a healthcare technology company that drives value through its unique combination of platforms, data, expertise, connectivity, and scale.
