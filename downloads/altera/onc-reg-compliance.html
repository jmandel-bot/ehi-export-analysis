ONC Certified Health IT Products Expand

A global healthcare IT innovator, Altera Digital Health Inc. develops and elevates technology to connect and inspire healthier communities. Our platform approach to our solutions is changing the way healthcare is delivered. By keeping the human user at the center of our design methodology, we are supporting caregivers and helping patients meet and exceed their wellness goals. Together, with our clients, we’re driving a new era of healthcare.

Altera is delighted to offer products on the Certified Health IT Product List (CHPL). CHPL is the authoritative and comprehensive listing of Health IT certified through the Office of the National Coordinator (ONC) for Health Information Technology Certification Program.

21st Century Cures Final Rule Communications Compliance Expand

At Altera, we take regulatory compliance very seriously. As such, we outline below the communications guidelines that comply with the ONC’s 21st Century Cures Act final rule.

Should there be any restriction that contravenes the following communications restrictions in our contracts, we will not enforce them. Moreover, the next time a contract is renewed, any such restrictions (if present) will be removed.

Altera will never prohibit or restrict any communication regarding:

  • The usability of our products
  • The interoperability of our products
  • The security of our products
  • Relevant information regarding users’ experiences when using our products
  • Altera business practices related to exchanging electronic health information
  • The manner which a user of an Altera product has used it
File ONC Information Blocking Concern Expand

 

Altera is fully committed to supporting an interoperable healthcare environment, including partnering with clients, policy makers and other stakeholders to ensure information exchange occurs where appropriate. We are committed to addressing any concerns about information blocking, whether from clients or those submitted by others in a position to interact with Electronic Health Information documented in and exchanged from Altera technologies. If you would like to share feedback with Altera or request follow-up, please complete Contact Us form at the bottom of the page and someone will contact you to gather more details in our effort to assess the concern and resolve where necessary.

NOTE: Altera clients should avail themselves of our Client Support process or their Account Executive team if they have concerns or questions about something that might fall under the information blocking regulation. We will be happy to address any issues or questions in that way, as all Altera staff have undergone training on this important regulation.

Paragon

  1. Click on the links below to download the EHI Export Documentation files to your local machine.

  3. Click on the links for each field to know more about them
