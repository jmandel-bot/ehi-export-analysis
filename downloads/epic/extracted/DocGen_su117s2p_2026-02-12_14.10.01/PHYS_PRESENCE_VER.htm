PHYS_PRESENCE_VER
Description:
This table contains a row for each time a user was confirmed to be physically present in a patient's home.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 PAT_ENC_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 CM_CT_OWNER_ID VARCHAR No
The Community ID (CID) of the instance that owns this contact. This is populated only if you use IntraConnect.
6 METHOD_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The method of physical presence verification
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
NFC Scan In
NFC Scan Out
Start Visit
End Visit
Other NFC Scan
7 VERIFICATION_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
The instant of verification of physical presence
8 PRESENT_USER_ID VARCHAR No
The user who was physically present at the patient's home.
9 PRESENT_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
10 LATITUDE NUMERIC No
This item stores the latitude of the user's mobile device at the time of physical presence verification.
11 LONGITUDE NUMERIC No
This item stores the longitude of the user's mobile device at the time of physical presence verification.
12 POS_LATITUDE VARCHAR No
The latitude of the location used for electronic visit verification.
13 POS_LONGITUDE VARCHAR No
The longitude of the location used for electronic visit verification.
14 POS_ADDR VARCHAR No
The address string of the location used for electronic visit verification.
15 ACCURACY NUMERIC No
The radius of uncertainty associated with the location coordinate captured at the time of physical presence verification, measured in meters. A higher value means lower accuracy of the retrieved coordinate.
16 POS_STREET_ADDRESS VARCHAR No
The street address of the location used for electronic visit verification.
17 POS_CITY VARCHAR No
The city of the location used for electronic visit verification.
18 POS_STATE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The state of the location used for electronic visit verification.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
19 POS_ZIP VARCHAR No
The ZIP code of the location used for electronic visit verification.
20 POS_COUNTY_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The county of the location used for electronic visit verification.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
21 POS_COUNTRY_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The country of the location used for electronic visit verification.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
22 MANUAL_ENTER_TM_YN VARCHAR No
This column stores if the time log for the physical presence verification event was manually entered by the user.
May contain organization-specific values: No

No
