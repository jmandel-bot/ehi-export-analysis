AUDIOGRAM_METADATA
Description:
Contains values for metadata related to an Audiogram Exam. Including Technique, Transducer, Reliability, Baseline, and RSH ID.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
FINDING_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 FINDING_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the finding record.
2 AUD_BASELINE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Item used to indicate what type of baseline an audiogram is. Options are: none, left, right, bilateral
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Right Baseline
Bilateral Baseline
Left Baseline
3 AUDIO_RELIABILITY_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Represents how reliable the Audiogram results are. The possible responses are Good, Fair, or Poor.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Good
Fair
Poor
