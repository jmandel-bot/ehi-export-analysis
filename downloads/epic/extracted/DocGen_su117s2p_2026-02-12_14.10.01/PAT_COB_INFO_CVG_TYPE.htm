PAT_COB_INFO_CVG_TYPE
Description:
The type of coverage stored on the Coordination of Benefits (COB) information in the patient record.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ID 1
GROUP_LINE 2
VALUE_LINE 3

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
The line number of the associated coordination of benefits coverage type in the patient's record. This forms the foreign key to the PAT_COB_INFO table.
2 GROUP_LINE INTEGER No
The line number of the associated coordination of benefits coverage type in the patient's record. Together with PAT_ID, this forms the foreign key to the PAT_COB_INFO table.
3 VALUE_LINE INTEGER No
The line number of one of the multiple coordination of benefits coverage types associated with the patient from the PAT_COB_INFO table.
4 COB_INS_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Coordination of benefits insurance coverage types.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
