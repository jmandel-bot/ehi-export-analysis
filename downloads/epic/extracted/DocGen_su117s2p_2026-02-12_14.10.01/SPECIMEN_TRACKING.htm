SPECIMEN_TRACKING
Description:
Contains the post resulted tracking information about specimens.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
SPECIMEN_RECORD_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 SPECIMEN_RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the finding record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 TRACKING_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Contains the current status for tracking a specimen after it's been resulted.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
No Treatment Required
Patient/Caregiver Needs to be Contacted
Patient/Caregiver Informed
Schedule Follow-up
Schedule Procedure
Refer Mohs/Surgeon
Follow-up Arranged
Treatment Complete
Unable to Contact Patient
Patient Refused
Refer for Excision
Refer to Resident Surgery Clinic
Refer to Other Specialties
Awaiting Additional Workup
4 TRACKING_COMP_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores whether this specimen is considered complete with respect to tracking after the specimen is resulted.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Completed
Not Tracked
5 POST_RESULT_DX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Contains the diagnosis used for tracking purposes after the specimen has been resulted. Not to be used as a clinical diagnosis.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
BCC
BCC, Superficial
SCC
SCC in Situ
Melanoma
Melanoma in Situ
Other Skin Cancer
Other Malignant Neoplasm
Atypical Nevus - Mild
Atypical Nevus - Moderate
Atypical Nevus - Severe
Actinic Keratosis
Other Benign Neoplasm
Other Skin Eruption
Neoplasm of Uncertain Behavior
Other
6 TRACKING_DESC_ID VARCHAR No
Contains the a summary/comment about the specimen tracking done after the specimen has been resulted.
7 TRACK_ORD_ID NUMERIC No
The order that the tracking data relates to. This is a virtual item linked to other rows containing the same information. Future: This column should be deprecated after the conversion from DLG 2154560 is complete.
8 TRACKING_ORDER_ID NUMERIC No
The order that the tracking data relates to.
