SOCIAL_HX_ALC_USE
Description:
The SOCIAL_HX_ALC_USE table contains social alcohol history data entered in clinical system patient encounters. Note: Typically, only the most recent social history data for a patient is extracted. However, you may elect to extract all social history contacts if you wish. In this case, since one patient encounter may contain multiple social history contacts, each contact is uniquely identified by a combination of the patient encounter serial number and a line number.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
A unique serial number for this encounter. This number is unique across all patients and encounters in the system.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The contact date on which the history information was collected.
4 ALCOHOL_DRINKS_WK VARCHAR No
Stores the alcohol history of drinks per week.
5 HX_DRINK_TYPES_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the type of alcohol consumed for alcohol history.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
