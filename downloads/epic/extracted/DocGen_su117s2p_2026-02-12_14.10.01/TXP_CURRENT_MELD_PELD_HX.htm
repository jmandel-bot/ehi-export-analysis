TXP_CURRENT_MELD_PELD_HX
Description:
This table contains the liver score history for a transplant episode. After 6/28/2022 exceptions will not be included due to changes in the OPTN liver allocation policy.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the episode record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 CURRENT_SCORE INTEGER No
The most advantageous active liver score. After 6/28/2022 exceptions will not be included due to changes in the OPTN liver allocation policy.
4 CURRENT_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The status, when it is the most advantageous active liver score.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
One A
One B
5 CURRENT_SCORE_TYPE VARCHAR No
The type of liver score that this line represents. That is, "MELD", "PELD" or "STATUS"
6 CURRENT_EXCEPTION_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The exception for this line. After 6/28/2022 exceptions will not be included due to changes in the OPTN liver allocation policy.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
7 CURRENT_START_DATE DATETIME No
Start date of the associated score.
8 CURRENT_END_DATE DATETIME No
Expiration date of the associated score.
9 CUR_EXCEPTION_OTHER VARCHAR No
The other exception for the meld score. After 6/28/2022 exceptions will not be included due to changes in the OPTN liver allocation policy.
10 SCORE_HISTORY_LINE INTEGER No
Line in the HSB 30400 related group corresponding to this line in score history
