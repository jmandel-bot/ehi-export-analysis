DENTAL_VISIT_INFO_HX
Description:
The DENTAL_VISIT_INFO_HX table contains the edit history of a dental visit record.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
REGIMEN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 REGIMEN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique ID of the visit record for this row.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 VISIT_MOD_INST_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
The date and time the visit was modified.
4 VISIT_MOD_USER_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID associated with the user record that modified data for a visit.
5 VISIT_MOD_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
6 VISIT_LINKED_CSN NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
7 TREATMENT_HX_ID NUMERIC No
The revision history of the treatment ID containing the visit.
8 VISIT_SPAC_DAYS_HX INTEGER No
The revision history of the requested spacing, in days, for a visit.
9 VISIT_SCHED_INST_HX VARCHAR No
The revision history of the scheduling instructions for a visit.
10 VISIT_APPT_LEN_HX INTEGER No
The revision history of the requested appointment length for a visit.
11 VISIT_APPT_HX NUMERIC No
The revision history of the appointment linked to a visit.
12 VISIT_NUMBER_HX INTEGER No
The revision history of a visit's order within a dental treatment.
13 VISIT_CHRON_INSTANT_DTTM DATETIME (Attached) No
This column records the date and time of a documentation change to the dental visit within the dental treatment plan.
