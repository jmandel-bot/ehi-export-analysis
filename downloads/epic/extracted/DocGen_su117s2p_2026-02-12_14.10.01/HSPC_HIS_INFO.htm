HSPC_HIS_INFO
Description:
Contains information about Hospice Item Sets, such as the status, reason for record, and version.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
DATASET_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 DATASET_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the data set record.
2 DATASET_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the dataset, formatted as the patient's name and the reason for record.
3 INSTANT_NOADD_EDIT_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
The date and time that data that is not specific to a contact was edited for a data set.
4 NOADD_ITEMS_EDITED VARCHAR No
Data that is not specific to a contact that was edited for a data set.
5 DATASET_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This item contains the current submission status of the Hospice Item Set.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
New
Visit Complete
Marked for Submission
Submitted
Accepted
Rejected
Voided
In Progress
Ready for 3rd Party
Sent to 3rd Party
6 DATASET_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores what type of dataset the record is. Currently datasets can be OASIS or HIS.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
OASIS Dataset
HIS Dataset
HOPE Dataset
7 HIS_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the reason for record (e.g. Admission or Discharge) of the HIS Dataset. This corresponds to item A0250 on the Hospice Item Set.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Admission
Discharge
HUV 1
HUV 2
8 HIS_VERSION_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Version of Hospice Item Set to use.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
1.00
2.00
3.00
9 HIS_EPISODE_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier of the hospice episode of care record associated with an HIS data set record.
