HH_MEDS_ADDL_INFO
Description:
The HH_MEDS_ADDL_INFO table contains information specific to Home Health/Hospice medications. This includes the hospice coverage status of a medication.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORDER_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ORDER_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the order record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 PAT_ENC_CSN NUMERIC No
The item stores the patient (EPT) contact serial number (CSN) associated with the update to the order's additional info.
4 ENTRY_EMP_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the user that updated an order's additional info
5 ENTRY_EMP_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
6 ENTRY_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Stores the instant that the additional information was synchronized from the Remote Client
7 HSPC_CVG_STAT_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the hospice coverage status of a medication which helps to determine if the Hospice is responsible for paying for a medication
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Hospice Covered
Not Hospice Covered
8 TAKING_DIFF_POC_YN VARCHAR No
Stores whether or not for this order (ORD) that the patient taking different sig should be pulled on to the Plan of Care (POC).
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
9 HSPC_NONCVRD_MED_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This column contains the reason why a medication is not covered by the hospice benefit.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Other
10 HSPC_NONCVRD_MED_RSN_C_CMT VARCHAR No
This column contains the comment for the reason why a medication isn't covered by the hospice benefit.
The category values for this column were already listed for column: HSPC_NONCVRD_MED_RSN_C_NAME
11 WHICH_SIG_ON_POC_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This item stores which sigs to show on the plan of care.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Prescribed Sig
Taking Differently Or Not Taking
Both
