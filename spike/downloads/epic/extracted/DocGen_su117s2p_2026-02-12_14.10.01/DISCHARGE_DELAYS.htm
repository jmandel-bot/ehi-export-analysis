DISCHARGE_DELAYS
Description:
Holds the discharge delays on a patient.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the patient record for this row. This column is frequently used to link to the PATIENT table.
4 PAT_ENC_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
5 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
6 DD_DELAY_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Discharge delay
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Patient refusing to leave
Clinical state deteriorating
Waiting on specialist consultation
Waiting on lab result
Waiting on destination approval
Administering IV medications
Patient's ride delayed
Need physician signoff
7 DD_ADDED_AT_DDTM_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
Instant discharge delay was added
8 DD_RESOLVED_AT_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
When the discharge delay was resolved
9 DD_COMMENT VARCHAR No
Holds the comment entered by users on a delay
10 DD_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Holds the current status of a delay
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Active
Resolved
Discontinued
11 DD_IS_PRIMARY_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether the delay is primary. Y indicates that the delay is primary. A null value indicates that the delay is not primary. An N will not be populated for this column.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
12 DD_ADDED_AT_DDTM_LOCAL_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Local instant discharge delay was added
13 DD_RESOLVED_AT_LOCAL_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
When the discharge delay was resolved in the local time zone
