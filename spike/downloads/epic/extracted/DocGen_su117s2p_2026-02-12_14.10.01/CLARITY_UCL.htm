CLARITY_UCL
Description:
Universal Charge Lines (UCL) information. Each row represents one UCL transaction.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
UCL_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 UCL_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID associated with the charge record.
2 PATIENT_ID VARCHAR No
The patient the charge is associated with.
3 START_INST DATETIME (Local) No
The start date and time associated with the charge.
4 END_INST DATETIME (Local) No
The end date and time associated with the charge.
5 INSTI_PATIENT_ID VARCHAR No
Institutional Billing - linked patient
6 PROV_BILL_AREA_C NUMERIC No
The bill area for this charge.
7 PROV_BILL_AREA_C_BILL_AREA_NAME VARCHAR No
The record name of this bill area, financial subdivision, or financial division.
