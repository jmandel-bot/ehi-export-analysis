PAT_PREF_SERVICE_PROV
Description:
This table includes information about the service providers that are marked as patient preferred in Clinical Case Management's Continued Care and Services Coordination (CCSC) tool.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this contact. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this contact.
3 PAT_ENC_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
4 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
5 PROV_ID_PROV_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider. This item may be hidden in a public view of the CLARITY_SER table.
6 PROV_ADDR_ID VARCHAR No
The address ID of the service provider the patient has indicated they prefer for an encounter for the coordination type specified (in I EPT 97204). This item is populated for provider type (SER) service providers.
7 LOC_ID_LOC_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the revenue location.
8 DEPARTMENT_ID_EXTERNAL_NAME VARCHAR No
The external name of the department record. This is often used in patient correspondence such as reminder letters.
9 DP_COORD_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Coordination type of the service provider referenced in Pat Preferred Service Provider - SER ID (I EPT 97200), Pat Preferred Service Provider - EAF ID (I EPT 97202), or Pat Preferred Service Provider - EAF ID (I EPT 97203) that patient has indicated they prefer for an encounter.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Destination
Durable Medical Equipment
Dialysis/Infusion
Home Medical Care
Community Resources
Behavioral Health
Palliative Care
10 PAT_PREF_SVC_LN_ID NUMERIC No
Service line which the patient has indicated they prefer for a service provider.
11 PAT_PREF_SVC_LN_ID_SVC_LN_NAME VARCHAR No
The name (.2 item) of the service line record.
12 PAT_PREF_PARTNER_TIER_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The facility preferred partner type that this service provider is a part of at the time of choosing it as a patient preferred service provider.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Preferred
Partner
Hospital Preferred
Hospital Partner
ACO
Affiliated
Quality Designated Entity
Trusted Partner
