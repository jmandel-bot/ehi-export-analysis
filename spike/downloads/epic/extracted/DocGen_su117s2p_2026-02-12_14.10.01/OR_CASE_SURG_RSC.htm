OR_CASE_SURG_RSC
Description:
The OR_CASE_SURG_RSC table contains OR management system case surgeons/staff/resources.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
OR_CASE_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 OR_CASE_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the case record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The number of the lines of the surgical staff resource information in the case.
3 SRG_STF_RSC_ID_PROV_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider. This item may be hidden in a public view of the CLARITY_SER table.
4 START_OFFSET INTEGER No
The start time for the surgeon/staff/resource. It is measured in minutes relative to the beginning of the case.
5 END_OFFSET INTEGER No
The end time for the surgeon/staff/resource. It is measured in minutes relative to the beginning of the case.
6 REQUIRED_YN VARCHAR No
Yes/No flag which indicates whether the surgeon/staff/resource is required for the case.
