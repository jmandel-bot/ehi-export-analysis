CL_QANSWER_FAMILY_PROBS
Description:
This table stores information about the medical problems the patient's family members have that the patient entered when filling out their family history.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ANSWER_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ANSWER_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the answer record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 FAM_MEDICAL_HX_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The family medical history category ID for the associated family member.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
4 FAM_MEM_PROBLEM_ASSOC_MEM VARCHAR No
If the patient lists a family history problem, this is the unique identifier for the associated family member.
5 FAM_MEM_PROBLEM_PAT_HX_RESP_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The patient response category ID for the family member problem.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Yes
No
No Response
No (Not Pertinent)
6 FAM_MEM_PROBLEM_COMMENT VARCHAR No
Additional information the patient provided about the problem associated with their family member when they were filling out their family history.
7 FAM_MEM_PROBLEM_AGE_OF_ONSET INTEGER No
Approximate age that the problem was identified in the associated family member that was provided by the patient when they were filling out their family history.
8 FAM_MEM_PROBLEM_ONSET_AGE_END INTEGER No
The end of age of onset range, if age of onset was answered as a range.
