VAR_CONTACT
Description:
This table contains the contact specific information for each variance record.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
VARIANCE_ID 1
CONTACT_DATE_REAL 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 VARIANCE_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID for the variance record.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
The internal contact date for this variance record.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
4 IMPACT_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The impact rating for this variance contact.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
5 LAST_UPD_USER_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the user to last update this contact.
6 LAST_UPD_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
7 LAST_UPDATE_TIME DATETIME (Local) No
The instance this contact was last updated.
8 CONTACT_NUM VARCHAR No
Contact number for the variance contact.
9 INSTANT_OF_ENT_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
The instant of entry.
10 READING_CAREPLAN_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
Links to the care plan contact that this variance was documented under
11 VARIANCE_COMMENT VARCHAR No
The comment added to a variance on a care plan.
