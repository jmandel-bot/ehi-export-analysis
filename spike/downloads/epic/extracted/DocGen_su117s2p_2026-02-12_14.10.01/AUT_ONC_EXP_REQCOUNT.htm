AUT_ONC_EXP_REQCOUNT
Description:
The AUT_ONC_EXP_REQCOUNT table contains data about requested counts defaulting from treatment plans. The items here are stamps and live items to compare what the system thinks the value should be versus what users have inputted.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
AUTH_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 AUTH_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the authorization record.
2 NUM_SVCS_APPROVED INTEGER No
The number of services or units approved by the payor.
3 NUM_SVCS_REQUESTED INTEGER No
The number of services or units requested.
4 COVERAGE_ID NUMERIC No
The coverage associated with the authorization.
