CATARACT_PLANNING_IMPLANT
Description:
This table contains information about the lens that was implanted as part of cataract surgery.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 SUMMARY_BLOCK_ID NUMERIC No
This column stores the unique identifier for the episode record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 OPH_CAT_LENS_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Implanted lens type for cataract surgery
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Other
Standard
Toric
Multifocal
Multifocal Toric
Anterior Chamber
Sulcus
4 OPH_LENS_IMP_MODEL VARCHAR No
Implanted lens model
5 OPH_LENS_IMP_POWER VARCHAR No
Implanted lens power
6 OPH_LENS_IMP_AXIS VARCHAR No
Implanted lens axis
7 OPH_LENS_IMP_CMT VARCHAR No
Comments for the implanted intraocular lens
8 OPH_LENS_IMP_SUP_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the supply record entered into the implanted lens portion of cataract planning.
9 OPH_LENS_IMP_SUP_ID_SUPPLY_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the inventory item.
