CL_PAT_EDU_OS
Description:
This table stores the Patient Education information that is linked to a specific patient encounter and changes from one patient encounter to the next such as the Patient Education note for each encounter and when Patient Education was last documented for each encounter.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PED_ID 1
CONTACT_DATE_REAL 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PED_ID NUMERIC No
The Unique ID for the Patient Education Record.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
This is a numeric representation of the date of this encounter in your system. The integer portion of the number specifies the date of the encounter. The digits after the decimal point indicate multiple visits on one day.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The contact date in external format.
4 CONTACT_NUM INTEGER No
The contact number for the patient education record contact.
5 PAT_CSN NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
6 EDUCATION_NOTE_ID VARCHAR No
The unique ID of the note that is associated with this patient education contact.
7 INSTANT_OF_ENTRY DATETIME (Attached) No
Date/Time stamp for the instance when the when data was entered in a patient education contact.
8 INSTANT_OF_EDIT DATETIME (Attached) No
Date/Time stamp for the instance when the when data was edited in a patient education contact.
9 LAST_TAUGHT_AT DATETIME (Local) No
The instant when the patient education was last taught.
