PB_ACCT_AUTO_PAY_HX
Description:
The PB_AUTO_PAY_HX table stores the history of auto pay status for a premium billing account.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PB_ACCT_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PB_ACCT_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the account record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 AUTO_PAY_HX_ACT_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the auto pay action performed.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Enrolled
Canceled
Edited
4 AUTO_PAY_HX_ACT_SRC_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the source of the auto pay action performed.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Hyperspace
MyChart
Batch
Tapestry Link
SMS
Utility
5 AUTO_PAY_HX_EDIT_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
When a current auto pay setup is edited, this columns stores the thing that changed.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Payment Method
Payment Day
Payment Day and Method
Billing Email
6 AUTO_PAY_HX_MYPT_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the MyChart ID of the member if the auto pay action was performed from MyChart
7 AUTO_PAY_HX_ACT_DATE DATETIME No
Stores the date when a particular auto pay workflow was perfomed
8 AUTO_PAY_HX_TERM_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the reason as to why auto pay was terminated
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
User Canceled
Member Canceled
Payment Failed
No Current Coverages
Payment Method Expired
Employer Canceled
Government Withholdings
9 AUTO_PAY_HX_ACT_TM DATETIME (Local) No
This item stores the time when a particular auto pay workflow was performed
10 AUTO_PAY_HX_PMT_MTHD_ID NUMERIC No
Stores the payment method in place when a current auto pay setup is edited.
