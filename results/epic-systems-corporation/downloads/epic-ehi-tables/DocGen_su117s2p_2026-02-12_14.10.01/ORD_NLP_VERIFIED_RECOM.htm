ORD_NLP_VERIFIED_RECOM
Description:
This table contains information about general recommendations that were present on orders when the output from a natural language processing model to extract recommendations was verified or last modified.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORDER_ID 1
LINE 2
