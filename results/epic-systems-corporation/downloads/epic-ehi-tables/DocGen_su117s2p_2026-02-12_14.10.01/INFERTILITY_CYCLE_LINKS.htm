INFERTILITY_CYCLE_LINKS
Description:
The INFERTILITY_CYCLE_LINKS table contains information about patients participating in a fertility treatment cycle.
The INFERTILITY_CYCLE_LINKS table is not able to safely export all of its columns without potentially compromising the other patients that are attached to the infertility treatment cycle. We created the view V_EHI_ICF_CYCLE_PATIENTS_DATA to filter the INFERTILITY_CYCLE_LINKS data based on the extracted patient's roles (e.g., sperm donor, intended parent) on the infertility treatment cycle. See view on more information on how data is filtered.
