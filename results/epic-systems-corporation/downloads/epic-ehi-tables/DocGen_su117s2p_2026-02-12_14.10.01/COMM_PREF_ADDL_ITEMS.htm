8 DAILY_DIGEST_TM DATETIME (Local) No
The time at which the user wants to receive once daily To Do task reminder notifications. If the time zone column is populated, then the time is in that time zone. Otherwise, it is in UTC.
9 AUTO_ADD_TO_WAITLIST_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether an appointment will be automatically added to the Wait List upon scheduling based on the patient preference. 'Y' indicates that the appointment is auto-added when scheduling if the DEP/SDF settings allow for it. 'N' or NULL indicate that the appointment will not be auto-added.
The category values for this column were already listed for column: APPT_NOTIF_SCHEDULED_YN
10 RSLT_DAILY_DIGEST_YN VARCHAR No
This item stores whether the user has signed up to receive a Daily Digest tickler for test results.
