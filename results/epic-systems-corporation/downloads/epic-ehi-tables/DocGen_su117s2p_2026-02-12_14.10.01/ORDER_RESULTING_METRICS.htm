ORDER_RESULTING_METRICS
Description:
This table contains information about how your procedural orders were resulted.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORDER_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ORDER_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) of the order record associated with this procedure order.
2 TEXT_GENERATION_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of text generation statements used for study findings.
3 TEXT_GENERATION_EDIT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of edited text generation statements used for study findings. This is only possible with compact forms.
4 SUMMARY_STMT_ACTIVE_AUTO_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of active automatic summary statements generated from study findings and not removed by user.
5 SUMMARY_STMT_AUTO_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of automatic summary statements generated from study findings.
6 SUMMARY_STMT_ACTIVE_EDIT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of active edited summary statements that were not removed by the user.
7 SUMMARY_STMT_EDIT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of edited summary statements.
8 FINDINGS_SDE_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of SmartData elements documented on study findings.
9 INTRA_FINDINGS_INCLUDED_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of SmartData elements recorded by intraprocedural findings events in the procedural narrator that are included in final report.
10 INTRA_FINDINGS_REMOVED_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of SmartData elements recorded by intraprocedural findings events in the procedural narrator excluded from the final report.
11 VESSEL_ANNOTATION_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of vessel annotations documented in Study Review.
12 COMPACT_FORM_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether the order has been resulted with a Compact Form in Study Review.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
13 MEASUREMENT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of result components on the order.
14 CALCULATED_MEASUREMENT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of calculated result components on the order.
15 XML_MEASUREMENT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of measurements exchanged with measurement exchange on the order.
16 MODIFIED_XML_MEASUREMENT_COUNT INTEGER No
The count of exchanged measurements that were modified on the order.
17 ORDER_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The order type category number for the procedure order.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Medications

19 ACTV_EXCLUDE_FROM_CDS_REASON_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The Exclude From Decision Support reason for the order. It will be either 1 - Unsuccessful Attempt represents an order that was not successfully completed. or 2 - Documented on Wrong Patient represents the order's result information was documented on the incorrect patient.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:

Documented on Wrong Patient
20 TOTAL_STUDY_RVW_TIME INTEGER No
Total time study review was open for the study.
21 FINAL_PHYS_STUDY_RVW_TIME INTEGER No
Total time study review was open by the finalizing physician.
22 READ_PHYS_STUDY_RVW_TIME INTEGER No
Total time study review was open by reading physicians who are not the finalizing physician.
23 NON_READ_USER_STUDY_RVW_TIME INTEGER No
Total time study review was open by all users not listed as a reading physician.

Total number of SmartData elements documented on study findings by the finalizing physician.

Total number of SmartData elements documented on study findings by reading physicians who are not the finalizing physician.
