POC_VERSION_INFO
Description:
Overtime plan of care information.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
POC_ID 1
CONTACT_DATE_REAL 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 POC_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the plan of care record.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact. The digits after the decimal distinguish different contacts on the same date and are unique for each contact on that date. For example, .00 is the first/only contact, .01 is the second contact, etc.
3 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
4 CONTACT_SERIAL_NUM NUMERIC No
The contact serial number (CSN) of the contact.
5 CONTACT_NUM NUMERIC No
Stores contact number
6 CONTACT_COMMENT VARCHAR No
Describes the reason for creating the new contact
7 INSTANT_OF_ENTRY_DTTM DATETIME (Local) No
Stores the instant of entry when a record is edited
8 SPOC_EFF_FROM_DATE DATETIME No
The date when the plan of care is effective from.
9 SPOC_EFF_TO_DATE DATETIME No
The date when the plan of care is effective until.
10 END_AGE_MAJORITY_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether the effective to date for this plan is set to the patient's birthday when they achieve the age of majority.
May contain organization-specific values: No
