HSP_CLAIM_PAT_RESP
Description:
This table contains information about how the patient responsibility for the claim was calculated.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
CLAIM_PRINT_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 CLAIM_PRINT_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the claim print record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 CLAIM_PX_LINE_NUM INTEGER No
Stores the line number in related group 710 that this detail line corresponds to. If blank, then the patient responsibility detail on this line is for DRG reimbursement.
4 SERVICE_TYPE_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the service type associated with the benefits on this line. If coverage level benefits are used, then the service type will be blank.
5 SERVICE_TYPE_ID_SERVICE_TYPE_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of this benefit service type.
6 SERVICE_TYPE_SOURCE_DESC VARCHAR No
Stores a description of how the service type was calculated for the line.
7 DEDUCTIBLE_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount of deductible that was applied to this line.
8 COPAY_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount of copay that was applied to this line.
9 COINSURANCE_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount of coinsurance that was applied to this line.
10 NON_COVERED_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount that was not covered by insurance on this line.
11 NON_COVERED_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the reason that the not covered amount is not covered by insurance.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Noncovered procedure
Lifetime insurance maximum reached
Annual insurance maximum reached
Visit insurance maximum reached
12 ANNUAL_MOOP_CONTRIB_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount that this line contributed to the maximum out of pocked accumulation.
13 VISIT_MOOP_CONTRIB_AMOUNT NUMERIC No
Stores the amount that this line contributed to the maximum visit out of pocked accumulation.
14 OUT_OF_POCKET_LMT_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Stores the reason that the out-of-pocket maximum limited the patient responsibility for the current line.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Annual out-of-pocket max met on a previous line
Visit out-of-pocket max met on a previous line
Annual and visit out-of-pocket max met on a previous line
Annual out-of-pocket max met on the current line
Visit out-of-pocket max met on the current line
Annual and visit out-of-pocket max met on the current line
