ORGAN_EXTRA_VESSEL
Description:
Extra vessels used during transplant.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORG_RECORD_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ORG_RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the organ record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 VESSEL_DUID VARCHAR No
Donor ID from the United Network for Organ Sharing (UNOS) of the extra vessel used in the transplant.
4 VESSEL_PLACEMENT_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The location of any extra vessels used during transplant.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Infra Renal Aortic Jump Graft
Supraceliac Aortic Jump Graft
Iliac Artery Jump Graft
Interposition Graft
Donor Iliac Bifurcation Graft
Renal Vein Extension Graft
Portal Vein Extension Graft
5 VESSEL_COMMENTS VARCHAR No
Any comments about the donor vessels used during transplant.
6 VESSEL_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The type of donor vessels used during transplant.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Artery
Vein
