HSP_BDC_IMAGING
Description:
This table contains related imaging information for the Denial/Correspondence (BDC) master file.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
BDC_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 BDC_ID NUMERIC No
This column stores the unique identifier for the denial or correspondence record the related imaging information is associated with.
2 LINE INTEGER No
Line number of related imaging information that is being extracted by enterprise reporting.
3 BDC_IMAGE_MNE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The image mnemonic associated with this group of related denial/correspondence imaging information.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
4 IMAGE_KEY VARCHAR No
The image key associated with this group of related denial/correspondence imaging information.
5 IMAGE_PAGE_NUMBER VARCHAR No
The image page number associated with this group of related denial/correspondence imaging information.
6 BDC_PB_IMG_MNE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This column stores the professional billing image mnemonic that may be associated with this group of related correspondence imaging information.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
