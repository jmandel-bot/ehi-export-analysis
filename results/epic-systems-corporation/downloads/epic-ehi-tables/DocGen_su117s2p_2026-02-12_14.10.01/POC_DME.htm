POC_DME
Description:
This table contains durable medical equipment (DME) information for the Plan of Care (POC) master file.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
RECORD_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the plan of care record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 POC_DME_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This item contains the DME type.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
4 POC_DME_START_DT DATETIME No
This item contains the start date of episode DMEs as of the time the POC was saved.
5 POC_DME_END_DT DATETIME No
This item contains the end date of episode DMEs as of the time the POC was saved.
6 POC_DME_CMT VARCHAR No
This item contains the comments for episode DMEs as of the time the POC was saved.
7 POC_DME_DELETED_YN VARCHAR No
This item contains the deleted status for episode DMEs as of the time the POC was saved.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Yes
No
8 HSPC_COVERED_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The durable medical equipment - hospice coverage category ID for the plan of care.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Covered
Not Covered
9 HSPC_NONCVRD_RSN_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The durable medical equipment - hospice not covered reason category ID for the plan of care.
May contain organization-specific values: Yes
Category Entries:
Other

The durable medical equipment - hospice not covered reason category comment for the plan of care.
