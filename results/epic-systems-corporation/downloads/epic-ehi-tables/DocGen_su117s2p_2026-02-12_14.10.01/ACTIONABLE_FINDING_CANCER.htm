ACTIONABLE_FINDING_CANCER
Description:
This table contains information about what cancer was detected after an actionable finding was documented for the order.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
ORDER_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 ORDER_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the order record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 CANCER_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The type of cancer found by the incidental finding.
