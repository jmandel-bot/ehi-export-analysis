BENEFITS
Description:
The BENEFITS table contains information specific to benefits collection (BEN) record.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
RECORD_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the benefit record.
2 ENC_BENEFIT_VRX_ID NUMERIC No
The verification record for the encounter's benefits.
3 RECORD_CREATION_DT DATETIME No
The date the record was created.
4 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
Patient ID
5 VERIFICATION_DATE DATETIME No
The most recent verification date of this BEN record. This will be used to facilitate easier BEN record searching in the future.
6 ORIG_VERIFICATION_DATE DATETIME No
The original verification date of the data on this BEN record when it was entered manually or by RTE and verified for the first time.
7 RECORD_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The record's status.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Soft Deleted
Hidden
Hidden and Soft Deleted
8 BENEFIT_PERIOD_COVERAGE_ID NUMERIC No
Only populated on coverage-level benefits records. The record ID of the coverage associated with this benefit record.
9 BENEFIT_PERIOD_START_DATE DATETIME No
The start date for the benefits on this record. Only populated on coverage benefit records.
