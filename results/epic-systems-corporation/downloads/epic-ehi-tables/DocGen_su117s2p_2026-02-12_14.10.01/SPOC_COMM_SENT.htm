SPOC_COMM_SENT
Description:
This table contains information about communication sent for a plan of care.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
RECORD_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the plan of care record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 COMM_SENT_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The communication type category ID for the plan of care.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Letter
Call
Secure Chat
In Basket
MyChart
4 COMM_SENT_UTC_DTTM DATETIME (UTC) No
Stores the date the communication was sent
5 COMM_SENT_PROV_ID_PROV_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the service provider. This item may be hidden in a public view of the CLARITY_SER table.
6 LETTER_SENT_JOB VARCHAR No
Stores the Job ID of the letter communication sent
7 LETTER_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number of the patient encounter in which the letter was sent.
8 SENT_COMM_CMT VARCHAR No
The comment associated with the communication sent for the plan of care.
9 SENT_COMM_USER_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the user who sent the communication.
10 SENT_COMM_USER_ID_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the user record. This name may be hidden.
11 COMM_SENT_PAT_RELATIONSHIP_ID NUMERIC No
Stores the patient contact (RLA ID) who received the communication. Not used for the letter type.
12 COMM_SENT_MYPT_ID VARCHAR No
Stores the proxy (WPR ID) who received the communication. Not used for the letter type.
