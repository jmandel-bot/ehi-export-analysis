AP_CLAIM_IF_INFO_MOE
Description:
Claim level data that can be sent or received by prospective payment systems (PPS) pricers that use the Medicaid Outpatient Editor (MOE).

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
CLAIM_ID 1
LINE 2

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 CLAIM_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier (.1 item) for the claim info record.
2 LINE INTEGER No
The line number for the information associated with this record. Multiple pieces of information can be associated with this record.
3 MOE_RELEASE_VERSION VARCHAR No
Editor release version number applicable to this claim.
4 MOE_CLM_ERROR_NUM VARCHAR No
Number of claim-level edits returned.
5 MOE_DX_ERROR_NUM VARCHAR No
Number of diagnosis edits returned.
6 MOE_PX_ERROR_NUM VARCHAR No
Number of procedure edits returned.
7 MOE_TOTAL_ERRORS VARCHAR No
Total number of edits identified for this claim.
8 MOE_CLM_DISPOSITION VARCHAR No
Overall disposition of the claim, including claim-level, diagnosis, and procedure edits.
9 MOE_ERROR_DISP VARCHAR No
Claim disposition flag array; one flag for each disposition.
10 MOE_HI_CLM_ERR_DISP VARCHAR No
Highest claim-level edit disposition.
11 MOE_CLM_ERRORS VARCHAR No
Codes that describe any claim-level edits found by the editor.
