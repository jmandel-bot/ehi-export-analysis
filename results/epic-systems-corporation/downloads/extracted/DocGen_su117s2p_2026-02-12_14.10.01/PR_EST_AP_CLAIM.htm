10 MC_ANCHOR_CONTEXT INTEGER No
This is the Anchor context, meaning if there are mulitple lines in the related group that come from the same Anchor procedure this number points to the line that has the Anchor procedure. The Anchor procedure is what is shown to the member in Cost Calculator.
11 MC_SEARCH_CONTEXT INTEGER No
Holds a line number that corresponds to a line in RG PES 18180 which has the related search parameters that were in use when the detailed view was selected.
