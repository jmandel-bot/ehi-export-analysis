BEREAVE_FOL_INFO
Description:
This table contains information about your bereaved's follow-up record. These include bereaved record link, follow-up type, and follow-up form. The records included in this table are Bereavement Follow-Up (LHC) records.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
RECORD_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 RECORD_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the bereavement follow-up record.
2 RECORD_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The record status, such as active or soft-deleted.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Soft Deleted
Hidden
Hidden and Soft Deleted
3 BEREAVED_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the bereaved record linked to this bereavement follow-up.
4 PAT_CSN NUMERIC No
The contact serial number of the contact in a patient record in which the bereavement follow-up was documented.
5 FORM_ID VARCHAR No
The unique identifier of the custom interface (SmartForm) record that is used to document the bereavement follow-up.
6 FORM_ID_FORM_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the form associated with the questionnaire.
7 FORM_DAT VARCHAR No
The contact date (DAT) of the custom interface (SmartForm) record that is used to document the bereavement follow-up.
8 FOLLOW_UP_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Type of documentation in bereavement follow-up.

Category Entries:
Assessment
