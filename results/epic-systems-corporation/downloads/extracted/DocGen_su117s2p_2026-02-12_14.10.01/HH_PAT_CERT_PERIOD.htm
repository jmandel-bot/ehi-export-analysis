HH_PAT_CERT_PERIOD
Description:
This table contains information about Home Health and Hospice contacts, including the contact's visit set and certification period.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
PAT_ENC_CSN_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 PAT_ID VARCHAR No
Unique identifier for the patient.
2 CONTACT_DATE_REAL FLOAT No
Unique identifier for this contact for this patient.
3 CARE_PLAN_ID VARCHAR No
The care plan to which the contact is linked. Links to table CARE_INTEGRATOR.
4 CERT_PERIOD INTEGER No
The cert period to which the contact is linked.
5 VISIT_SET INTEGER No
The visit set of which the contact is a part.
6 PAT_ENC_CSN_ID NUMERIC No
The unique contact serial number for this contact. This number is unique across all patient encounters in your system. If you use IntraConnect, this is the Unique Contact Identifier (UCI).
7 CONTACT_DATE DATETIME No
The date of this contact in calendar format.
8 POC_ENC_NUM INTEGER No
The serial number of the plan of care encounter that the contact created.
9 CM_CT_OWNER_ID VARCHAR No
The Community ID (CID) of the instance that owns this contact. This is populated only if you use IntraConnect.
10 REPORTING_DISC_C_NAME VARCHAR No
This item stores the reporting discipline of the provider who completed the encounter.
May contain organization-specific values: No

Stores if the Plan of Care encounter's certification period has been deleted.
