AP_CLAIM_4
Description:
The AP_CLAIM_4 table contains one record for each claim in the managed care system's AP Claims module.

Primary Key
Column Name Ordinal Position
CLAIM_ID 1

Column Information
Name Type Discontinued?
1 CLAIM_ID NUMERIC No
The unique identifier for the claim record.
2 TOT_ADDL_ADJ NUMERIC No
Stores the total additional adjustments for the claim.
3 AP_CLAIM_IMPORT_SOURCE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
Used to define the type of source a claim is imported from to drive reporting on the different use cases a single workflow type may have.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Historical System
Repricing Source
Delegated Payer
Clearinghouse
Other Internal Source
Other External Source
4 SOURCE_GROUP_ID_LOC_NAME VARCHAR No
The name of the revenue location.
5 SERVICE_DATE_FROM_LINE_YN VARCHAR No
Indicates whether the service date was received at the header level, or was determined based on service lines.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
No
Yes
6 NCH_CLAIM_TYPE_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The National Claims History (NCH) code indicating the type of claim submitted.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Home Health Agency (HHA) claim
Non swing bed Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) claim
Swing bed Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) claim
Hospital Outpatient claim
Hospice claim
Inpatient claim
Inpatient Full Encounter claim
Local carrier non-durable medical equipment, prosthetics, orthotics, and supplies (DMEPOS) claim
Local carrier durable medical equipment, prosthetics, orthotics, and supplies (DMEPOS) claim
Durable medical equipment regional carrier (DMERC); non-DMEPOS claim
Durable medical equipment regional carrier (DMERC); DMEPOS claim
7 PAID_CLM_FILE_INTEREST_AMT NUMERIC No
The total interest amount from the file for an externally paid claim.
8 PAID_CLM_FILE_PENALTY_AMT NUMERIC No
The total penalty amount from the file for an externally paid claim.
9 BLK_DTA_MESSAGE_ID NUMERIC No
Contains the indexed AMS pointer to the last bulk data message record to impact this CLM.
10 REFUND_REASON VARCHAR No
The refund reason for the claim. Will be used on any refund letters generated for the claim.
11 PAYER_CLM_IDENT VARCHAR No
Stores the ID assigned to the claim from the payer. Currently in use for claims loaded through DINE.
12 AP_CLM_AR_STATUS_C_NAME VARCHAR No
The accounts receivable status of the AP claim.
May contain organization-specific values: No
Category Entries:
Not Ready for AR
Ready for AR
Released to AR
AR in Progress
AR Completed
Returning Funds
13 CLAIM_CHECK_MAIL_SENT_DATE DATETIME No
The date that the last check was mailed/sent or disbursed.
