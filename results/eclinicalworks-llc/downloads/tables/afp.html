Table afp
Description This table stores information related to Antenatal Fetal Protein (AFP) tests, which are used to screen for certain birth defects during pregnancy.
Column DataType Description
reportid int Unique identifier for each AFP report. It is a primary key for the labdata table.
insulinDependent smallint This column indicates whether the patient is insulin-dependent (1) or not (0).
GAweeks varchar Gestational age in weeks (e.g., 12, 24, 36).
GADays varchar Gestational age in days (e.g., 1, 15, 30).
GAdecimal varchar Gestational age in decimal format (e.g., 12.5, 24.3, 36.7).
GAdate varchar Date of gestational age calculation (e.g., 2021-01-01, 2021-06-15, 2021-12-31).
GAbyLMP smallint Indicates whether the gestational age is calculated by the last menstrual period (LMP) (1) or not (0).
lmpDate varchar Date of the last menstrual period (e.g., 2021-01-01, 2021-06-15, 2021-12-31).
GAbyUltrasound smallint Indicates whether the gestational age is calculated by ultrasound (1) or not (0).
ultrasoundDate varchar Date of the ultrasound (e.g., 2021-01-01, 2021-06-15, 2021-12-31).
GAbyDeliveryDate smallint Indicates whether the gestational age is calculated by the delivery date (1) or not (0).
deliveryDate varchar Date of delivery (e.g., 2021-01-01, 2021-06-15, 2021-12-31).
pregnancyType varchar Type of pregnancy (e.g., singleton, twins, triplets).
routineScreening smallint Indicates whether the AFP test is part of routine screening (1) or not (0).
neuralTubeDefects smallint Indicates whether the AFP test is for neural tube defects (1) or not (0).
advancedMaternalAge smallint Indicates whether the mother has advanced maternal age (1) or not (0).
downSyndrome smallint Indicates whether the AFP test is for down syndrome (1) or not (0).
cysticFibriosis smallint Indicates whether the AFP test is for cystic fibrosis (1) or not (0).
otherIndications smallint Indicates whether there are other indications for the AFP test (1) or not (0).
handWrittenInfo smallint Indicates whether the AFP report contains handwritten information (1) or not (0).
reasonElevated smallint Indicates whether there is a reason for elevated AFP levels (1) or not (0).
earlyGA smallint Indicates whether the gestational age is considered early (1) or not (0).
Hemolyzed smallint Indicates whether the blood sample is hemolyzed (1) or not (0).
weight varchar Patient's weight (e.g., 60, 70, 80).
race varchar Patient's race (e.g., Caucasian, African American, Asian).
