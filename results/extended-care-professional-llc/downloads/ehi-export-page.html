    Exporting Electronic Health Information (EHI)

    EHI can be exported from ECP to Excel or PDF through our User Interface. Information is available to be exported at the company, location, or resident level. See below for examples on how to export information at each level.

    Export at the Company-Wide Level

    To export at the company-wide level, click Executive Menu.

    Navigate to Reports

    Click the Reports tab. 

    Select Report Generator. The Report Generator Exec window will display.

    Select Locations

    Export the Report

    To export the report, click either Save to Excel or Save to PDF.

    Export at the Location Level

    To export at the location-level, click Admin Menu.

    Select the tab with the relevant information youâ€™d like to export (e.g. Reports).

    Select the report youâ€™d like to export (e.g. Vaccinations and Screenings).

    Click Open Report.

    Export the Report

    To export the report, click Export Options and select either Excel or PDF.

    Export at the Resident Level

    To export at the resident level, click an individual resident tile.

    Navigate to History Tab

    Click the History tab.

    Click the information youâ€™d like to export (e.g. Vitals History).

    Export the Report

    To export the report, select the desired date range and click either the PDF or Excel icon.
