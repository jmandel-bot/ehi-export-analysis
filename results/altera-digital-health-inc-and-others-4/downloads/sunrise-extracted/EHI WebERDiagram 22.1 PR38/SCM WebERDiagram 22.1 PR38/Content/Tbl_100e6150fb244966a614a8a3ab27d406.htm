dbo.SXACRCatalogTile 
Default Table Name  SXACRCatalogTile 
Definition  Catalog of Clinical Review Tiles. Each Clinical view contains one or more tiles. This table stores tile link to the TileDefault table, indicates how the tile should be presented to the user.
