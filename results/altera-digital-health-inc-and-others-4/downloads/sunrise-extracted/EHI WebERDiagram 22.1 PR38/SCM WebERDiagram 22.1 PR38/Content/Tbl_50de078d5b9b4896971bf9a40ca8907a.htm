dbo.SXASRGSurgicalItemAllergen 
Default Table Name  SXASRGSurgicalItemAllergen 
Definition  This table contains the allergen identifiers associated to a surgical item. Allergens are from the Allergen dictionary in Configuration Tools. Each surgical item may have one or more allergens.
