dbo.SXAMMGIChargeAlgorithmType 
Default Table Name  SXAMMGIChargeAlgorithmType 
Definition  This entity identifies further properties of the link between Generic Item and Charge Algorithm.
Is a child table to Generic Item Charge Algorithm. Further properties of the link include Patient Type and Outpatient Type.
