dbo.CV3OrderFieldState 
Default Table Name  CV3OrderFieldState 
Definition  Store the ‘state’ of order entry field when an order or order set is placed. A row will be created for an order entry field if any of its following states are altered by MLM: IsVisible, IsMandatory, and/ or IsReadOnly
