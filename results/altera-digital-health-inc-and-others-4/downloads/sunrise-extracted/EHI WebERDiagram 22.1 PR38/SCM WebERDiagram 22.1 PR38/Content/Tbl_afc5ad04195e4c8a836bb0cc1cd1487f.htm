dbo.SXAHMClientEvent 
Default Table Name  SXAHMClientEvent 
Definition  This Health Management patient data table stores the client events that are assigned to a patient. This table directly corresponds to the the rows displayed in the patient's schedule on the working view, summary view, or timeline view.
