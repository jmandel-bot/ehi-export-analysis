dbo.SXAAMRuleConditionDetail 
Default Table Name  SXAAMRuleConditionDetail 
Definition  When VWF is not installed at that time rule's conditions related to the rules are saved in this table. Every rule condition has Left Operator, operand and right operator associated with it. This table is in foreign key relationship with SXAAMRule table
