dbo.SXAESResourceCustomFieldCategory 
Default Table Name  SXAESResourceCustomFieldCategory 
Definition  Events, Resources and Locations are to have custom field categories defined against them that will be then presented to the user when booking an appointment. This table holds the custom field categories that are attached to specific resources.
