dbo.SXADMDiseaseQuestion 
Default Table Name  SXADMDiseaseQuestion 
Definition  Assignment of custom questions to diseases, with assignment-specific data such as IsRequired and DefaultValue. Also specifies global custom questions, which are implicitly mapped to every disease.
