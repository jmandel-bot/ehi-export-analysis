dbo.SXAAMREFIncomingReferral 
Default Table Name  SXAAMREFIncomingReferral 
Definition  This new table will be used to store incoming referrals. A referral is a recommendation of a medical or paramedical professional to provide a service to a Sunrise patient, so there may be many referrals/patient.
