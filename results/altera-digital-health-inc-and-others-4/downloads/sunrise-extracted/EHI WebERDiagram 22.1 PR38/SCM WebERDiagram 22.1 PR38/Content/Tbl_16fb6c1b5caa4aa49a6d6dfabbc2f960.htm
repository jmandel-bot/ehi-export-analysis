dbo.SXAAMBScriptFavoriteCategory 
Default Table Name  SXAAMBScriptFavoriteCategory 
Definition  For the Ambulatory Rx Writer, users will be given the ability to save their favorite prescriptions and organize them by category. This table will hold the user’s available categories.
