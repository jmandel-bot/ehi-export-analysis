dbo.SXAAMVisitRegDate 
Default Table Name  SXAAMVisitRegDate 
Definition  This table maintains various dates/times (partial and full) associated with visits. The various dates/times are of particular event enumeration
This table stores 17 different types of dates inclusing LOA check out and check in date. Comprehensive list as below:
1 Triage Date
2 Onset Date
3 Last Menstrual Period
4 Estimated Confinement Time
5 Treatment Start
6 Current Hospitalization Admit Date
7 Current Hospitalization Discharge Date
8 LOA Start Date
9 LOA Return Date
10 Similar Illness Date
11 Treatment End
12 Last XRay Date
13 Acute Manifestation Date
14 First Consult Date
15 Hearing and Vision RX Date
16 Date Last Worked
17 Date Authorized to Return to Work
