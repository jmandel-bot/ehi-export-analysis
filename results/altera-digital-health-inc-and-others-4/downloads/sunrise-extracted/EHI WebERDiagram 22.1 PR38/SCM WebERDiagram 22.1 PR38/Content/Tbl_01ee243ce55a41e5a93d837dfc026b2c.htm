dbo.CV3RxPolicyDtl 
Default Table Name  CV3RxPolicyDtl 
Definition  Defines the pharmacy policy details that can be associated to an order catalog item with a form of medication type. This controls the verification behavior of a pharmacy order and how warning messages are displayed.This table, together with CV3RxPolicy, will allow the site to define the locations at which an order catalog item requires pharmacy verification and whether the tasks are displayed on the worklist before or after verification
