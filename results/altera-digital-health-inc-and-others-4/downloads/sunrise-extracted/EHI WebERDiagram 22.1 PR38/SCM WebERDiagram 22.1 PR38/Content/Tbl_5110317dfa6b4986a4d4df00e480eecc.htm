dbo.SXAMMGIRestrictedInvestigational 
Default Table Name  SXAMMGIRestrictedInvestigational 
Definition  This entity contains details concerning the use of restricted/investigational generic items. Details include a list of care providers who are allowed to prescribe the restricted/investigational GI.
