dbo.SXACRTileGrowthChartDetail 
Default Table Name  SXACRTileGrowthChartDetail 
Definition  When new tiles are configured for a Clinical Summary View, information specific to each tile needs to be saved. This table holds the additional information involving Growh Charts.
