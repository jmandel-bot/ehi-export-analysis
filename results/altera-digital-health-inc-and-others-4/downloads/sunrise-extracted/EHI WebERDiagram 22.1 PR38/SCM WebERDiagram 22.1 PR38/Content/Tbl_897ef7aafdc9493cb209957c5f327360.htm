dbo.SXAGNWordSynonym 
Default Table Name  SXAGNWordSynonym 
Definition  Words that can be substituted with another word (synonym) when using special content search.If there are synonyms as blood and home the entries in the word synonym table will be like:
Blood Heme
Heme Blood
Blood Blood
Heme Heme.
