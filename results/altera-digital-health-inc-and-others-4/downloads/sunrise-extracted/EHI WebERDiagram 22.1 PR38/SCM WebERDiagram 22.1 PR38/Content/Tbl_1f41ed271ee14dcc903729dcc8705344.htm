dbo.SXAMMMedicationFormUnitofMeasureXRef 
Default Table Name  SXAMMMedicationFormUnitofMeasureXRef 
Definition  Provides a cross-reference between Medication Forms and Units of Measure.
In some cases, a Medication Form will "map" or "translate" equivalently to a Unit of Measure.
