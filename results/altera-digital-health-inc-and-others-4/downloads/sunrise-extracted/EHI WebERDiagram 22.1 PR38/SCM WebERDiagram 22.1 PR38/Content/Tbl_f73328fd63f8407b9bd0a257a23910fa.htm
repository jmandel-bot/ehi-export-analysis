dbo.SXAAMVisitBillCondition 
Default Table Name  SXAAMVisitBillCondition 
Definition  This table is an Xref between the CV3ClientVisit and the SXAAMVisitBillCondition . And also stores other Visit/Bill Condition related information.The codes are used to determine liability, coordinate benefits and administer clauses in benefit programs. Some will be predefined and unchangeable. The user will be able to add, modify, and delete user defined codes, but not system codes
