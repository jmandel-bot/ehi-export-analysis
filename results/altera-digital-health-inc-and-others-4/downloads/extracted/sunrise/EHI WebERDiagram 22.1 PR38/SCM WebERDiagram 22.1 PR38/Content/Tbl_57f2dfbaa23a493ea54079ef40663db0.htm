dbo.SXACCReportableConditionTriggerCode 
Default Table Name  SXACCReportableConditionTriggerCode 
Definition  SCM will require a set of trigger codes, that will be provided by the CDC. The RCTC are maintained by the CSTE / CDC Reportable Condition Knowledge Management System (RCKMS) Project. These trigger codes will be used in tests to determine if an eICR needs to be created. ATP will consume this list and provide to SCM.
