dbo.SXAESMCExceptionDefinitionAvailabilityLocationXREF 
Default Table Name  SXAESMCExceptionDefinitionAvailabilityLocationXREF 
Definition  The purpose of this table is to cross reference multiple locations of a Master Calendar Availability Exception Definition.
