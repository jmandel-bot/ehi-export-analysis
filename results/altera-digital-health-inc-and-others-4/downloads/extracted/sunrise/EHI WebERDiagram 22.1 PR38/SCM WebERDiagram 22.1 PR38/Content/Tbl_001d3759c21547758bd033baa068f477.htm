dbo.SXAHMEventOccurrenceRule 
Default Table Name  SXAHMEventOccurrenceRule 
Definition  Defines the completion rules for each occurrence of a series, specifying when the occurrence should be completed relative to a reference date, such as date of birth, as well as the spacing between this occurrence and the next occurrence in the series. These rules are used to calculate when the next occurrence should be administered, or the completion schedule for the entire series.
