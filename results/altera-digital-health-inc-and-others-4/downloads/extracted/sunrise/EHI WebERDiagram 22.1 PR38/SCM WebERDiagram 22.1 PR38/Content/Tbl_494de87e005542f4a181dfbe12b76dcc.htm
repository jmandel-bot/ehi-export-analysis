dbo.CV3ResultAcknowledgementPolicyDtl 
Default Table Name  CV3ResultAcknowledgementPolicyDtl 
Definition  Stores the detailed information about a result acknowledgement Policy. This identifies which location(s) require result acknowledgement and who should acknowledge the result.
